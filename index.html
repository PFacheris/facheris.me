Major Projects

ReelTime

HackNY 2013 24Hr hack that "technically" supports infinite users for WebRTC based video chat and allows for synchronized, download-less watching of video file from one user's computer. Functions using websockets and adding of file chunks on the fly to html5 video player. Inspired by wanting to easily watch TV/Movies with my long distance girlfriend.

May be further developed when I have some free time.

What2DoCU

Finds and ranks events on college campuses based on number of facebook friends from your school attending. Makes use of Node.js and Socket.io for real-time user interaction. Created in one night during HackColumbia 2013 and winner of "Best-Use of Student Affairs API."

Ctrl-F

Universal package tracker built entirely with Javascript (Node.js and Backbone.js)
